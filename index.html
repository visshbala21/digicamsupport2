Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I use The DigiCam?

Open the app, grant camera permissions, and start capturing stunning photos with customizable filters.

2. How can I save my photos?

Tap the save button after taking a photo to store it in your gallery under the "DigiCam" album.

3. I found a bug, how do I report it?
